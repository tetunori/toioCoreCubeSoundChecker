toio™Core Cube Sound Checker
MIDI音源などを使ってtoio™Core CubeのSound機能をチェックできるシンプルツール
A simple tool enables us to check Sound function of toio™Core Cube by using MIDI audio source and so on.
1. Connect toio™Core Cube.
2-1. Test a single note.
57(= A4)
2-2. Select/drag & drop a MIDI audio file.
